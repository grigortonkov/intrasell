How do I make a Wishlist?

To create a Wishlist you must first create a smartefox.com account if you don't already have one.
If you already have an account, just login to your account. LOGIN >>

Go to any part of the smartefox.com site. When you find a product that you want to add to your Wishlist, click the Wishlist link



Save Your Shopping Basket?

To Save Your Shopping Basket you must first create a smartefox.com account if you don't already have one.
If you already have an account, just login to your account. LOGIN >>

Go to Shopping Basket and click on the Save this Basket link.
